Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

Booking & Reservations

Booking a tour is easy! Simply browse our tour listings, select your desired tour, choose your preferred dates, and follow the booking process. You'll need to provide your personal details and payment information to complete the reservation.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and PayPal. For some tours, we also accept bank transfers. Payment details will be provided during the booking process.

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking, but please note that cancellation policies vary by tour. Most tours allow free cancellation up to 30 days before departure. For specific details, please refer to the tour's cancellation policy or contact our customer service.

Tour Information

Tour inclusions vary by package, but typically include accommodation, transportation, guided activities, and some meals. Specific inclusions are listed in each tour's description. Please review the details carefully before booking.

We provide a detailed packing list for each tour in your confirmation email. Generally, we recommend comfortable clothing, appropriate footwear, weather-appropriate gear, and any necessary personal items. Don't forget your travel documents and medications!

We offer tours suitable for various age groups and fitness levels. Each tour description includes information about physical requirements and age recommendations. If you have specific concerns, please contact us for advice on choosing the right tour.

Travel & Safety

Yes, we strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance for all our tours. It should cover medical expenses, trip cancellation, lost luggage, and other potential issues. Some tours require proof of insurance before departure.

Your safety is our top priority. All our tours follow strict safety protocols, and our guides are trained in first aid and emergency procedures. We regularly update our safety measures based on local conditions and global best practices.

We have comprehensive emergency response plans for all our tours. Our guides are trained to handle various situations, and we maintain 24/7 support for emergencies. You'll receive emergency contact information before your tour begins.
